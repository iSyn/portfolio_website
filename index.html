September 18, 2017

More Ore v0.6.1

So... took a little break from coding for little bit more than a week. I was losing my motivation and drive and slowly lost interest in More Ore. I made a small post to /r/incremental_games asking what people thought and it went extremely well!
To my surprise, everyone was extremely nice and positive. This was also my first time showing off my game to a somewhat large audience. Everyone gave me their input and feedback and I responded to mostly every comment.
According to Google Analytics, I had a whopping 1,200 users visit the site and mess around with whatever they liked. I really enjoyed this experience as well as talking with others with similar interests.
I made a Trello account to keep track of progress as well as ideas to implement in the future to streamline my development progress.
Safe to say, the fire is back! Excited to code yet again!

September 4, 2017

More Ore v0.6 - Vignettes are cool

Alright that was hard... Spent yesterday re-writing all my code to be neater and more functional. Saving now finally works which is so amazing. Buttons used to expand on hover but complications came when the player wanted to purchase multiple of the same item. I in turn switched to a tooltip type display similiar to cookie clicker. I also removed the upgrades tab as it was annoying to switch back and forth from the store tab to the upgrades tab. Instead, I put upgrades at the top of the store.
